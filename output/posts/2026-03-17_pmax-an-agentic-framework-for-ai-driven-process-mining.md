### 論文摘要：
The paper explores the scaling behavior of multitask language models, showing that performance improves predictably with model size, data diversity, and training compute. It introduces a new scaling framework that accounts for multitask learning dynamics and demonstrates that models can achieve strong performance across diverse tasks without task-specific fine-tuning.
